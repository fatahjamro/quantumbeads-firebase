Research That Informs Our Work

Our consulting and training are grounded in active academic research

Why This Matters

Our founder's PhD research explores advanced mathematical structures in quantum information theory and their applications in hardware benchmarking, quantum cryptography, and error correction. This ongoing work directly informs the consulting and training we provide — giving clients access to expertise at the frontier of quantum computing, not just textbook knowledge.

An Overview of Quantum Latin Squares in Quantum Information Theory
Abdul Fatah, Ian McLoughlin, Saim Ghafoor, Iulia Anton
Atlantic Technological University Galway, Ireland
2024 International Conference on Quantum Communications, Networking, and Computing (QCNC), Kanazawa, Japan
IEEE Proceedings, pp. 57-64

A comprehensive overview synthesising current knowledge on quantum Latin squares, exploring their theoretical foundations, known constructions, and applications in quantum error correction, mutually unbiased bases, and unitary error bases.

About QuantumBeads

Pioneering quantum computing research and education

Our Mission

QuantumBeads exists to make quantum computing accessible and understandable. We help organisations cut through the hype, understand what quantum can (and can't) do for them, and build real quantum capabilities through training and consulting.

Our Approach

We combine active PhD-level research with practical, jargon-free communication. Whether you're a company exploring quantum for the first time or a team looking to upskill, we meet you where you are.

Where We're Headed

As our PhD research matures, we're working towards developing practical tools for quantum hardware verification and benchmarking. Today, our focus is on helping you build the knowledge foundation to be quantum-ready.
