January 17-18, 2024

International Winter School 2024 on Quantum Computing Technologies

Event Overview

A landmark international event bringing together quantum computing researchers, practitioners, and enthusiasts from around the world.

Event Details

Venue: National Centre for Robotics and Automation: Condition Monitoring Systems (NCRA-CMS) Labs

Location: Mehran University of Engineering & Technology (MUET) Jamshoro, Pakistan

Duration: 2 days intensive workshop

Participants: International quantum computing researchers, students, industry professionals

What to Expect

  • Expert-led sessions on quantum computing fundamentals and advanced topics
  • Hands-on practical workshops on quantum algorithm implementation
  • Keynote presentations from leading quantum computing researchers
  • Networking opportunities with quantum computing professionals worldwide
  • Access to cutting-edge research and verification tools
  • Certification of participation

Target Audience

  • PhD students and researchers in quantum computing
  • Industry professionals working on quantum technologies
  • Students interested in quantum computing careers
  • Quantum computing enthusiasts and learners

About NCRA-CMS Labs

The National Centre for Robotics and Automation: Condition Monitoring Systems Labs at MUET is a state-of-the-art research facility dedicated to advancing automation and robotics technologies, providing an ideal environment for collaborative quantum computing research and training.

Available Workshop Topics

We offer workshops on the following topics, customised to your team's needs and experience level:

  • Introduction to Quantum Computing — for teams new to quantum
  • Quantum Algorithms & Programming — hands-on coding with quantum circuits
  • Quantum Error Correction — understanding noise and fault tolerance
  • Quantum for Business Leaders — strategic overview without the jargon

Each workshop can be delivered remotely or in person, for half-day, full-day, or multi-day formats.
